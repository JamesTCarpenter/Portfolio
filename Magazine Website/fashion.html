Beyonce X Adidas Superstar Platform: Hit or Miss?

We all know Beyonce has domitated the music industry for a number of years but are her new collaberation with Adidas a work of art or a deformation of a classic shoe?

This shoe release is not the first time Beyonce has released clothing and footwear with Adidas. Her collection IVY PARK was launched a while ago but there seems to always be one piece that is a bit off the mark. At the beginning of this year she released her range that included a colour scheme that was mocked by social media for looking identical to the Sainbury's work uniform. Although this isn't as extreme, is it worth discussing.

The shoes themselves seemed to sell out in minutes but is this just due to the name behind the product or is it a genuine appreciation of the modern twist to a Superstar silouette? In my opinion the platform sole with a sharp front seems to take away the point of a Superstar which is meant to be chunky and round, however maybe I am an anomoly with my view and it deservs the credit the selling figures are showing.

As always, leave a comment on our Twitter letting us know what you think.

London Fashion Week Continues Online As Burberry Film In Countryside

Although we all seem to be on hold in some way due to lockdown, it seems that fashion is unable to stop.

This is especially true for London Fashion week which is one of the most popular events in the industry. Just because fshion experts and enthusiasts are unable to go to the catwalks and fashion houses it hasn't stopped the continuation of this yearly event, which is now being done online.

Maybe one of the most impressive online fashion shows this year came from Burberry, who live streamed the models walking in the seasons newest garments, all from what appeared to be a forest in the countryside. As well as the clothes being stylish and in season, it was nice to see that the brand were trying something different, and maybe subconsiously trying to distract us from the distinct closed environment we are currently living in due to the pandemic, even if it was only for a litle while.
